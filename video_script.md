## POST-PRODUCTION NOTES

### Editing Tips
- **Transitions:** Use smooth fades (0.3s) between scenes
- **Text Overlays:** Add key metrics as text overlays
- **Music:** Subtle background music (optional, low volume)
- **Pacing:** Keep it fast-paced but clear
- **Zoom:** Zoom in on important UI elements

### Audio
- **Voiceover:** Clear, enthusiastic, professional tone
- **Volume:** Consistent throughout
- **Background:** Minimal or no background music
- **Timing:** Sync voiceover with visual actions

### Visual Effects
- **Highlights:** Use subtle glow/outline on clickable elements
- **Animations:** Smooth transitions, no jarring cuts
- **Text:** Large, readable fonts for metrics
- **Colors:** Consistent with FocusFlow brand

### Export Settings
- **Format:** MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution:** 1920x1080
- **Frame Rate:** 30 FPS
- **Bitrate:** 8-10 Mbps
- **Audio:** AAC, 192 kbps
